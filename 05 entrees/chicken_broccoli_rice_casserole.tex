\begin{quote}
This casserole builds flavor in layers, starting with pressure-cooked chicken that produces tender meat and a flavorful broth used to enrich the cream sauce. The rice is cooked al-dente and finishes in the oven while absorbing the sauce. Broccoli is blanched briefly to maintain its structure and bright color through the final bake. Corn is caramelized to bring out its natural sweetness, onions are softened, then garlic and mushrooms are added to build a savory base. The sauce brings everything together: a spiced roux (rosemary, paprika, nutmeg) built with the reserved chicken broth and milk, then finished with cream cheese for richness and tang. All components are combined and baked to meld. Crispy fried onions are added near the end to preserve their crunch and add textural contrast.
\end{quote}

\section*{Directions}

\noindent
Preheat oven to \textit{375°F} ---
Prepare \textbf{chicken~broth}: whisk 3~Tbsp. \textbf{Better~Than~Bouillon} into 4~cups \textbf{water} until dissolved; set aside in \textit{Medium Bowl~\#1} ---
Chop \textbf{broccoli}; set aside in \textit{Medium Bowl~\#2} ---
Drain \textbf{corn}; set aside in \textit{Small Bowl~\#1} ---
Dice \textbf{onion}; set aside in \textit{Small Bowl~\#2} ---
Mince \textbf{garlic} and drain \textbf{mushrooms}; combine in \textit{Small Bowl~\#3} ---
Soften \textbf{cream cheese} at room temp and cube; set aside in \textit{Small Bowl~\#4} ---
Grease a 9~inch×13~inch baking dish with \nicefrac{1}{2}~Tbsp. \textbf{butter}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item Place \textbf{chicken~thighs} in Instant Pot. Add 2~cups \textbf{chicken~broth} (\textit{Medium Bowl~\#1}), 1~tsp. \textbf{thyme}, and one \textbf{bay~leaf}. Cook on \textbf{high pressure} for \textit{15~minutes} and release after \textit{5~minutes}.
	
	\item Set cooked \textbf{chicken} aside on a plate; let rest for \textit{5-10~minutes}, then pick and dice meat, discarding bones and skin. Place diced \textbf{chicken} in \textit{Large Bowl~\#1}. Strain cooked broth and reserve in \textit{Medium Bowl~\#4}. 

    \item Cook \textbf{rice} al-dente using 2~cups \textbf{chicken~broth} (\textit{Medium Bowl~\#1}) and a \textbf{bay leaf}. Stop your rice cooker 5-10 minutes early.
    
    \item While rice cooks, bring a pot of salted water to boil. Blanch \textbf{broccoli} for \textit{2~minutes}, then immediately strain in a metal colander rinsing with cold water. Drain well and transfer to a large bowl.
    
    \item In a large skillet, melt 4~Tbsp. \textbf{butter} on medium heat. Add 2~tsp. \textbf{oil} and \textbf{corn} (\textit{Small Bowl~\#1}) and increase heat to medium-high to brown and caramelize the corn, about \textit{5~minutes}. Add diced \textbf{onion} (\textit{Small Bowl~\#2}) and cook until translucent, \textit{5-10~minutes}. Add \textbf{garlic} and \textbf{mushrooms} (\textit{Small Bowl~\#3}), cooking for another \textit{5~minutes}. Remove from heat and transfer to \textit{Large Bowl~\#2}; set aside.
    
    \item In a large saucepan, melt 2~Tbsp. \textbf{butter}. Add 1~tsp. \textbf{rosemary}, 1~tsp. \textbf{paprika}, \nicefrac{1}{4}~tsp. \textbf{MSG}, \nicefrac{1}{2}~tsp. \textbf{garlic powder}, \nicefrac{1}{2}~tsp. \textbf{onion powder}, pinch \textbf{nutmeg}, 1~tsp. \textbf{salt}, \nicefrac{1}{2}~tsp. \textbf{black pepper}, and \nicefrac{1}{4}~tsp. \textbf{red pepper}. Heat on medium, stirring constantly for \textit{30~seconds}. Sprinkle 3~Tbsp. \textbf{flour} over the butter/spice mixture using a whisk to combine. Cook, stirring constantly for \textit{2~minutes}.
    
    \item Gradually whisk in 2~cups of \textbf{reserved~broth} (\textit{Medium Bowl~\#4}), then 2~cups \textbf{milk}. Heat and whisk constantly until bubbling and thickened. Add cubes of \textbf{cream cheese} (\textit{Small Bowl~\#4}) and cut/whisk until melted and smooth. Remove from heat and transfer to \textit{Large Bowl~\#3}; set aside.
    
    \item In \textit{Large Bowl~\#4}, combine and mix blanched \textbf{broccoli} (\textit{Medium Bowl~\#2}), cooked \textbf{rice}, cooked \textbf{vegetables} (\textit{Large Bowl~\#2}), diced \textbf{chicken} (\textit{Large Bowl~\#1}), and \textbf{sauce} (\textit{Large Bowl~\#3}).
    
    \item Transfer mixture to prepared baking dish and bake at \textit{375°F} for \textit{20~minutes}. Remove from oven to sprinkle liberally with \nicefrac{1}{4}~cup \textbf{crispy fried onions}, and bake another \textit{10~minutes}. Rest \textit{5-10~minutes} before serving.
\end{enumerate}
